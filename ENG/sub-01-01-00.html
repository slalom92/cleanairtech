Meanign of the CI

Cymbal mark "the art of making clean air"

Clean Air Tech's symbol symbolizes the vision of creating a healthy life for workers with technology to create clean air. "CLEAN" represents the purification of the dust collector "AIR" represents clean air and the atmosphere "TECH" represents the technology to make it. With "AIR" in the middle and "CLEAN" and "TECH" on the left and right, it means that humans are the center and core of the product and technology.
