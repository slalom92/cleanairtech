Features

  • DUST COLLECTOR WITH ULTRA NANO FILTER DEVELOPED BY
    INDEPENDENT PATENT

    • The filter surface is equipped with an ultra-nano filter coated with nano-fiber.
    • The filtration efficiency (99.1%@0.3μm) is so high that fine dust and fume can be removed.
    • It has about twice as long life as a polyester filter.
    • Polyester Filter : less than 2 years
    • Membrane Filter : less than 3 years
    • Ultra Nano Filter: Less than 4 years

  • Air Pulse Jet

    • Clean the filter by spraying compressed air stored in the air tank at regular intervals.
    • The filter can be cleaned while the dust collector is operating. (operate 24 hours continuously)
    • The filter is equipped with a venturi that increases efficiency when cleaning by spraying compressed air.

  • German Analog Differential Pressure Gauge

    • Equipped with an analog differential pressure meter from AFRISO, a world-class measuring instrument manufacturer in Germany It has less residual failure and enables precise measurement.
    • The differential pressure gauge determines how clogged the filter is and tells you when to replace it.

  • High efficiency turbofan

    • High-efficiency, durable turbofan for reliable and excellent suction.
    • The impeller is ultra-precise electron balancing to minimize vibration and noise.

Option

  • Combo Panel

    • Change the analog control panel to a digital controller.
    • You can determine the degree of clogging of the filter by displaying the differential pressure of the filter digitally.
    • There is a remote control function that allows you to control the dust collector in conjunction with other equipment.

  • CE

    • CAPU meets all requirements of the EU Council Directive on safety, health, the environment and consumer protection This product has been certified as CE certification. Optionally, you can apply CE authentication.

  • One-touch detachable dust box

    • Change the heavy and uncomfortable drawer-type dust box to a dust box that can be easily removed with one touch.
    • There is a window to check how much dust has been accumulated, and the wheels are convenient to empty the dust box.

  • Hopper, Valve

    • Change the dust box to a funnel-shaped structure. Store large amounts of dust.
    • The rotary valve or butterfly valve can be used to empty the dust.

  • GDC-TEX Filter

    • Option to change to membrane coating filter.

    Explosion-proof construction

    • options for applying explosion protection.

  • Bean-vent Structure

    • Option to change bottom to flange shape.

    Flame arrester

    • Add a device to prevent fire by extinguishing flames during inhalation.

  • Customizing

    • Design changes or new developments can be made to specifications other than standard products upon request.

    Power specification change

    • 220V / 380V / 440V, Single Phase / Three Phase, 50Hz / 60Hz available.
